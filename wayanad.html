About Wayanad

Wayanad is a **scenic hill station** in Kerala, known for its **lush greenery, waterfalls, caves, and wildlife sanctuaries**. It is a paradise for **nature lovers, trekkers, and wildlife enthusiasts**.

Wayanad at a Glance

Famous For: Wildlife, Trekking, Waterfalls, Tea & Coffee Plantations
District: Wayanad
Best Season to Visit: October – May
Weather: Summer 20-30°C, Winter 15-25°C

How to Reach Wayanad

By Air

The nearest airport is **Calicut International Airport (CCJ)**, around **90 km** away.

By Train

The nearest railway station is **Kozhikode (Calicut) Railway Station**, **85 km** away.

By Road

Wayanad is well-connected by road from **Kozhikode, Mysore, and Bangalore**.
