KOZHIKODE (CALICUT)

About Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known as **Calicut**, is a historic city that played a major role in the **spice trade**. It is where **Vasco da Gama first landed in India in 1498**, marking the beginning of European exploration in India.

Kozhikode at a Glance

Famous For: History, Beaches, Malabar Cuisine, Spices
District: Kozhikode
Best Season to Visit: October – March
Weather: Summer 27-35°C, Winter 22-30°C

How to Reach Kozhikode

By Air

The nearest airport is **Calicut International Airport (CCJ)**, located **28 km from the city center**.

By Train

Kozhikode Railway Station is a major stop in the Southern Railway network.

By Road

Connected via **NH-66 and NH-766**, linking it to Kochi, Bangalore, and Mangalore.
