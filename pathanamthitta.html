PATHANAMTHITTA

About Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is famous for its **spiritual significance, lush greenery, and scenic rivers**. It is home to the **Sabarimala Temple**, one of the largest annual pilgrimage sites in the world.

Pathanamthitta at a Glance

Famous For: Temples, Rivers, Pilgrimage, Forests
District: Pathanamthitta
Best Season to Visit: September – February
Weather: Summer 24-36°C, Winter 20-30°C

How to Reach Pathanamthitta

By Air

The nearest airport is **Trivandrum International Airport (TRV)**, around **113 km** away.

By Train

Closest railway stations: **Thiruvalla (30 km)**, Chengannur, and Kollam.

By Road

Well-connected by roads to **Trivandrum, Kochi, and Kottayam**.
