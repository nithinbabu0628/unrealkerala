KASARAGOD

About Kasaragod

Kasaragod is known for its **majestic forts, pristine beaches, backwaters, and unique culture**. It is home to the famous **Bekal Fort**, the largest in Kerala, and offers stunning coastal views.

Kasaragod at a Glance

Famous For: Forts, Beaches, Backwaters, Temples
District: Kasaragod
Best Season to Visit: September – March
Weather: Summer 24-34°C, Winter 20-30°C

How to Reach Kasaragod

By Air

The nearest airport is **Mangalore International Airport (IXE)**, around **70 km** away.

By Train

Kasaragod has its own railway station, well connected to major Indian cities.

By Road

Kasaragod is well-connected by road to **Mangalore, Kannur, and Bangalore**.
