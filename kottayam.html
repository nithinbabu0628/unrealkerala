KOTTAYAM

About Kottayam

Kottayam, known as the **"Land of Letters, Latex, and Lakes"**, is a beautiful district in Kerala. It is home to lush green landscapes, backwaters, rubber plantations, and significant historical and religious sites. As a major educational hub, Kottayam was the first district in India to achieve 100% literacy.

Kottayam at a Glance

Famous For: Backwaters, Rubber Plantations, Churches, and Hill Stations
District: Kottayam
Best Season to Visit: October – February
Weather: Summer 25-35°C, Winter 20-25°C

How to Reach Kottayam

By Air

The nearest airport is **Cochin International Airport (COK)**, located about **85 km** away.

By Train

Kottayam Railway Station is well connected with major cities like Kochi, Trivandrum, and Bangalore.

By Road

Well-connected by road with National and State Highways. Regular KSRTC and private buses operate to major cities.

Top Attractions in Kottayam

Vembanad Lake

The longest lake in India and the heart of Kerala’s backwaters, Vembanad Lake is famous for houseboat cruises and the **Nehru Trophy Boat Race**.
